Hey, I'm Zhelin 👋

I'm a Senior Staff Research Scientist at Epsilon, Publicis Groupe in Chicago, where I design and ship AI-powered bidding and optimization systems running across thousands of ad campaigns — improving client revenue, reach, and efficiency at scale.

I hold a PhD in Mechanical Engineering, an MS in Applied Mathematics, and an MS in Mechanical Engineering — all from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). My doctoral research on optimal control earned a Best Paper Award (top 9 of 504 submissions). I also served as an Adjunct Research Assistant Professor at UIUC, mentoring graduate students and publishing on control theory and robotic applications.

This site is where I step out of the lab and share what I'm learning, building, and thinking about — from hands-on AI projects you can actually use, to books that shifted how I see the world, to raising my little one with curiosity and intention in an AI-shaped future.
